Workout Plan for BMI Below Normal


Cardiovascular Exercise:

  • Day 1: 20 minutes of brisk walking
  • Day 2: 20 minutes of cycling
  • Day 3: 15 minutes of light jogging

Strength Training - Bodyweight Exercises:

  • Day 1: Bodyweight squats (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Day 2: Push-ups (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Day 3: Plank exercise (hold for 30 seconds, repeat 3 times)


Workout Plan for Normal BMI


Cardiovascular Exercise:

  • Day 1: 30 minutes of running or brisk walking
  • Day 2: 20 minutes of cycling
  • Day 3: 30 minutes of swimming or other aerobic activity

Strength Training:

  • Day 1: Squats (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Day 2: Push-ups (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Day 3: Dumbbell Rows (3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm)


Workout Plan for BMI in Overweight Range


Cardiovascular Exercise:

  • Day 1: 30 minutes of brisk walking or cycling
  • Day 2: 20 minutes of high-intensity interval training (HIIT)
  • Day 3: 30 minutes of swimming or other aerobic activity

Strength Training:

  • Day 1: Squats (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Day 2: Push-ups (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Day 3: Dumbbell Rows (3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm)

Additional Activities:

  • Day 4: 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio (e.g., brisk walking)
  • Day 5: Bodyweight exercises (e.g., lunges, planks)


Workout Plan for BMI in Obese Range


Cardiovascular Exercise:

  • Day 1: 45 minutes of brisk walking or cycling
  • Day 2: 30 minutes of swimming or water aerobics
  • Day 3: 20 minutes of high-intensity interval training (HIIT)

Strength Training:

  • Day 1: Bodyweight squats (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Day 2: Modified push-ups (elevated or against a wall) (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Day 3: Seated leg press or chair squats (3 sets of 10-12 reps)

Flexibility and Mobility:

  • Every Day: Include dynamic stretches and yoga poses for flexibility and mobility.

Additional Activities:

  • Day 4: 30 minutes of low-impact cardio (e.g., stationary cycling)
  • Day 5: Bodyweight exercises for the upper body (e.g., seated dips, arm circles)
